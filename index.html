Our Mission

The Team

Zero Industries is a team of nine high school sophomores. Together, to provide a solution to the obnoxiously boring car ride between Los Angeles and Las Vegas, we designed a theme park based ironically on Transportation.

The Park

Zero is a theme park based on transportation. To provide our visitors with a relaxing, educational experience, our attractions are styled after historical, modern, and futuristic methods of transit, ranging from horseback to planes to jetpacks.

Attractions

Blast Off

Blastoff simulates a ride into space. Soon after the 400 foot peak is reached, the trip takes a turn for the worse, as it plummets down towards Earth.

Hyperloop

An innovative method of transporation, reaching speeds up to 400 mph, before slowing down and making a loop around the park, giving the passengers a preview of the park.
