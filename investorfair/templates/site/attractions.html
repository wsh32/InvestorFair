Blast Off!close

Embark on a trip to space, including a true zero-gravity experience! Blastoff simulates a ride into space, as it rockets up to its 300 foot high apex. As it nears its peak, the roller coaster decelerates to simulate zero gravity. However, soon after the apex is reached, the trip takes a turn for the worse, as it plummets down towards earth. After many twists and turns, the spaceship finally crash-lands into water, and the crew is safely rescued.

The Hyperloopclose

An innovative method of transporation, the Hyperloop provides its passengers with a ride to and from Las Vegas. Reaching speeds up to 400 mph, the Hyperloop is able to make the 60 mile journey in 15 minutes, before slowing down and making a loop around the park, giving the passengers a preview of the park.
